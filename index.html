About Windek Group

Nigeria's Integrated Industrial Powerhouse

Windek Group is a diversified Nigerian conglomerate delivering end-to-end industrial solutions that power national development. We operate across four high-impact verticals — Energy Infrastructure, Procurement & Contracting, Logistics & Supply Chain, and Food Distribution.

Founded on the principles of excellence and innovation, Windek Group has grown to become a trusted partner for government agencies, multinational corporations, and development organizations across Nigeria and West Africa.
